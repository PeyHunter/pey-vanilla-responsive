local Nørrebro

Nørrebro is a dynamic and multicultural neighborhood located in the northern part of Copenhagen. It is known for its lively atmosphere, trendy bars, diverse food scene, and cultural attractions. Nørrebro has a strong sense of community and activism, and it offers visitors a taste of Copenhagen's diverse and cosmopolitan character. Nørrebro offers a wide range of food and drink options, including street food, food markets, trendy eateries, and popular bars and breweries. From traditional Danish dishes to international flavors, the neighborhood has something for everyone to explore and enjoy.

Places to drink

Pompette is a loveable Nørrebro wine bar serving natural wines that will leave you feeling tipsy. Paloma Vermuteria, named after a Mexican cocktail, serves up delicious summer drinks that pack a punch. People Like Us, a socially conscious brewing company founded by brothers Lars and Jesper Carlsen, work with a team solely comprised of socially marginalized individuals.

Places to visit

Jærgsborgsgade, Blågårdsgade, and Assistens Kirkegård are all located in the Nørrebro district of Copenhagen, Denmark. Jærgsborgsgade is a popular street known for its trendy shops, cafes, and restaurants. Blågårdsgade is a multicultural street with a mix of shops, restaurants, and bars. Assistens Kirkegård is a famous cemetery where several famous Danes are buried, including author Hans Christian Andersen and philosopher Søren Kierkegaard. It is also a popular park where locals go for walks and picnics.
